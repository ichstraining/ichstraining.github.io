pets Cat Team Training Instructions

Now that you've reviewed the procedures for Dog Team Training, let's review the Cat Team Training module.

The Cat Team Training consists of 3 modules. Review all the interactive eLearning modules below carefully.

  • Module 1 outlines the steps to clean the cat cages.
  • Module 2 outlines the steps to clean the cat colony room.
  • Module 3 outlines the steps to clean the cat isolation room.

Each section is followed by a short quiz. Take a screenshot of your quiz results and email them to the shelter manager. This is proof that you have successfully completed the section. Once you have passed the quiz for section one with a 90% or higher you can move on to the next section.

Click on the headers to expand or collapse each section.
