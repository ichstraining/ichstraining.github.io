pets Pet Adoption Training Instructions

Welcome to the Animal Adoption Training page!

The Animal Adoption process consists of 2 modules. Review all the interactive eLearning modules carefully.

  • Module 1 asseses your current knowledge of the animal adoption process (approximately 10 minutes).
  • Module 2 begins your Animal Adoption Training (approximately 30 minutes)

Be sure to take a screenshot of your test results at the end of Module 2, then email your results to the shelter manager. This is proof that you have successfully completed the modules.

Click on the headers to expand or collapse each module.
